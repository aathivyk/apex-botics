Provincial Excellence

Apex Botics was founded by Aathi following a significant achievement at the provincial level in a Canadian engineering competition.

By implementing Simultaneous Localization and Mapping (SLAM), Aathi developed an autonomous system that successfully navigated complex environments, earning top honors for technical innovation and programming precision.

Now based in Redmond, WA, Aathi is dedicated to bringing this high-level engineering logic to the next generation of students through hands-on, hardware-first learning.
