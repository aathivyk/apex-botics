APEX ALPHA

The Gateway to Autonomous Engineering

Course Description

Apex Alpha is designed as an entry point into the world of robotics. We move past basic consumer electronics to understand the "brain" of a robot—the ESP32 microcontroller. This course balances theoretical hardware logic with hands-on building, ensuring a solid foundation before advancing to mobility.

What You Will Achieve

  • Mastery of circuit breadboarding and signal flow.
  • Proficiency in basic C++ programming specifically for hardware.
  • Ability to interface with environmental sensors to trigger real-world actions.
  • Completion of a fundamental logic-based project.
