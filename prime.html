APEX PRIME

Autonomous and Artificial Intelligence

Course Description

The pinnacle of the program, Apex Prime, introduces Simultaneous Localization and Mapping (SLAM). Students move into true autonomy where the robot makes decisions based on mapped environments. This level represents professional-grade engineering challenges.

What You Will Achieve

  • Implementation of complex 5-sensor SLAM navigation.
  • Advanced data filtering for smooth, high-speed autonomous runs.
  • System-wide troubleshooting of interconnected hardware components.
  • The ability to design a robot that "knows" where it is without human input.
